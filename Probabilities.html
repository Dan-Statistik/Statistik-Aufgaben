Fracht-Aufgabe:

A large freight elevator can transport a maximum of 9800 kg.

Suppose a load of cargo containing boxes must be transported via the elevator.

Experience has shown that the weight of boxes of this type of cargo follows a distribution with mean Âµ = 205 and standard deviation s = 15 kg.

a) Determine the maximum number of boxes which can be loaded and transported with a probability of at least 90%.


# R Code to solve the problem
# Given values
capacity <- 9800
mu <- 205
s <- 15

# Desired probability (at least 90%)
desired_prob <- 0.9

# Calculate the z-score corresponding to the desired probability
z_score <- qnorm(desired_prob)

# Calculate the maximum number of boxes
max_boxes <- floor((capacity - mu) / (s / sqrt(z_score^2)))

# Print the result
cat("The maximum number of boxes that can be loaded and transported with a probability of at least 90% is:", max_boxes)
